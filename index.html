About The Barber

Kristine Roccia

The Barber at BMG (Monroe County Airport) is a full service barbershop that offers a relaxing atmosphere with consistent haircuts and the best view from a barber chair there is to offer! With over 10 years experience, as a licensed cosmetologist and barber, I’m confident in providing a variety of services for you and tailored to you. My favorite is cutting, but I got you covered on shaves, beard trims, highlights and color services too!
